# Corporate AI Solutions — Creator Style

_Last updated: 2026-04-21 (seeded from messaging-reframe artifacts — needs validation against winning content)_

## Voice calibration

- **Direct over warm** — we respect the reader's time
- **Concrete over abstract** — numbers, artifacts, specific names
- **Confident, not swaggering** — the math does the work
- **Operator voice, not vendor voice** — sounds like a builder talking to builders

## Sentence patterns

- Short sentence leads. Longer sentence explains. Fragment for punch.
- Lead with the claim. Then the receipt.
- Comparison via dash — old vs. new, them vs. us.

## Rhythm rules

- Vary sentence length aggressively. 4 words. Then 22.
- Never three long sentences in a row.
- Use fragments deliberately — once per paragraph max.

## Words we use

- factory, inventory, portfolio, compound, unit economics
- ship, built, shipped (past tense dominant — shows receipts)
- operator, builder, founder
- margin, cost structure, cycle time

## Words we avoid

- leverage, solution, empower, seamless, robust
- cutting-edge, next-generation, best-in-class
- "we believe" (just state it)
- "at the end of the day" / "to be honest" (filler)
- "democratise" (overused in AI)
- unicorn (as an aspiration — only as the factory metaphor)

## Opening patterns that work

- State the contradiction: "Everyone says X. The math says Y."
- Drop a number: "38 platforms. $5K each. 7 days."
- Name the reframe: "The unicorn is the factory, not the product."

## Closing patterns that work

- Point to the math, not the vision
- Call out what the reader does next (not generic "learn more")
- End on the artifact: "Built. Shipped. Running."

## Sounds like us vs. doesn't

✅ "38 platforms built. 10 hitting $1M ARR = $60-100M portfolio."
❌ "We leverage AI to empower founders with cutting-edge solutions."

✅ "Individual exits are optional liquidity events."
❌ "We're excited to announce our strategic approach to monetisation."

✅ "The factory is the moat."
❌ "Our proprietary platform creates defensible competitive advantages."
