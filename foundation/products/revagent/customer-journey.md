## Stall points

### Stall #1: "Is RevAgent vaporware?"
- Where: first touch
- What's happening: claim is big (replace PM + GTM org), bar is "show me receipts"
- What unblocks it: live dashboard of actual RevAgent output; case study per pilot product

### Stall #2: "Does it work for products that aren't generator/SaaS?"
- Where: evaluation
- What's happening: RevAgent patterns learned on portfolio may not generalise
- What unblocks it: be explicit about the product archetypes it handles well vs. poorly

### Stall #3: R&D Tax Tracker — "Swanson Reed already owns this"
- Where: R&D pilot evaluation
- What's happening: TaxTrex (2018) is the incumbent
- What unblocks it: position as AI-native vs. survey-based; emphasize consultancy white-label channel (Swanson Reed doesn't offer that)

### Stall #4: "Free trial → paid conversion for consultancies"
- Where: 2026-07 cliff (free tier ends)
- What's happening: consultancies used it free for EOFY, now have to decide
- What unblocks it: by 2026-06-30, each consultancy has 5+ client reports generated and tracked ARR contribution — make retention obvious
