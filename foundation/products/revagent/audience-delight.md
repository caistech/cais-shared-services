# RevAgent — Audience Delight

_Last updated: 2026-04-21 (seeded from memory:project_revagent — needs raw source once RevAgent has early users)_

## Primary audience

**Solo founders and small studio operators who have built multiple AI products but can't monetise them.**

Profile:
- Ships fast. Has 3-20+ platforms / side projects / side businesses.
- Each product has some users or a demo or traction signal — none have hit $1M ARR.
- Bottleneck is NOT building. Bottleneck is positioning, pricing, channel, running GTM.
- Doesn't want to hire a marketing team. Either can't afford it or doesn't believe in it.
- Does believe in AI agents replacing functions. Already uses Claude / Cursor / Codex daily.

Inspired by the "Austin Lau at Anthropic" archetype — one non-technical operator + AI workflows = output of a full growth org. RevAgent is that, productised.

## Secondary audiences

- **Agency owners with productised services** wanting to spin out products
- **Indie hackers with portfolios** (the #buildinpublic crowd past the "first product" stage)
- **Corporate intrapreneurs** running internal product portfolios

## What lights them up

- "Factory economics" — concrete unit math, not platitudes
- Seeing someone else's portfolio numbers (radical transparency, Austin Lau-style)
- AI agents that do the boring parts (ads, outreach, SEO monitoring) without hand-holding
- Exit stories at 6-10x revenue (not unicorn exits — reachable outcomes)
- Killing channels that don't work — permission to stop trying
