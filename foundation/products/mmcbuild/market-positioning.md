# MMC Build — Market Positioning

_Last updated: 2026-04-21 (migrated from memory:client-intel.md, seeded from mmcbuild.com.au analysis)_

## Core narrative

**Australia's first AI-powered NCC compliance platform.** Compliance is the wedge; the pipeline (Comply → Build → Quote → Directory → Train) is the moat.

## Territory we own

- **NCC compliance as AI-native workflow** — first mover in AU residential construction
- **Monash University "Kit of Parts" / "System 600" integration** — academic/industry partnership
- **Sequential pipeline vision** — not standalone modules; each stage feeds the next
- **Council-ready output** — PDF exports with NCC citation, not generic AI summaries

## Territory that's contested

- "AI for construction" — generic claim. Lead with compliance specificity.
- "Modern Methods of Construction" — industry-wide term. Own it via Monash partnership, not the term itself.
- "Sustainable construction" — crowded. Anchor in specific outcomes (60% time reduction, 40% waste reduction).

## Territory we've ceded (intentionally)

- Generic AEC software (Procore, Aconex territory)
- BIM/CAD tooling
- On-site coordination apps
- International markets (AU-first, AU-native NCC focus)
