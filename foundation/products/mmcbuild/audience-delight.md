# MMC Build — Audience Delight

_Last updated: 2026-04-21 (seeded from client-intel + website analysis — needs raw source material for vocabulary)_

## Primary audience

**AU residential builders and small-to-mid construction firms** who are:

- Paying compliance consultants $2-5K per project for NCC sign-off
- Losing jobs because compliance reports take weeks
- Interested in MMC (modular, CLT, prefab) but don't know how to cost or spec it
- Not "tech-forward" — need tools that feel like construction, not SaaS

## Secondary audiences

- **Architects** — want compliance input earlier in design phase
- **Developers** — want cost/compliance certainty before DA submission
- **Councils** — receive the PDF output (not buyers, but quality gatekeepers)

## What lights them up

- Compliance reports that read like what a senior consultant would write
- "Council-ready" output — no rework required
- Clear material substitution suggestions with real cost deltas
- Partnership credibility (Monash, HIA, ABCB, Master Builders)
