# MMC Build — Creator Style

_Last updated: 2026-04-21 (seeded — needs validation against any existing MMCBuild content)_

## Voice calibration

- **Professional, not casual** — these are licensed builders and architects
- **Construction-first, AI-second** — AI is the tool, not the pitch
- **Concrete outcomes** — time saved, dollars saved, plans approved
- **Regulatory-confident** — NCC citations, council-ready, no hedging on compliance claims

## Sentence patterns

- Lead with the builder's problem
- Short practical statements
- Technical terms (NCC, BAL, BCA) used naturally, not explained
- Minimal "we" — more "your plans, your project, your council"

## Words we use

- plans, project, council, approval
- NCC, BCA, BAL, fire rating, structural, waterproofing
- compliance, report, citation
- time, cost, rework
- Modern Methods of Construction, MMC, CLT, prefab, modular

## Words we avoid

- factory (conflicts with parent brand; also means something literal in construction)
- inventory, portfolio, studio, venture
- "democratising construction" or similar vendor-speak
- "AI-powered" in H1s — lead with outcome, AI is implementation
- "empower," "leverage," "seamless," "robust"
- "unicorn" in any form

## Opening patterns that work

- Name the pain: "Your compliance consultant takes 3 weeks."
- Name the outcome: "Council-ready NCC report. 10 minutes."
- Name the credential: "Built with Monash University's Kit of Parts team."

## Closing patterns that work

- Practical CTA: "Upload your plans. Get your report."
- Pricing anchor: "From $149/month."
- Credential close: "Trusted by builders across NSW, VIC, QLD."

## Sounds like MMC Build vs. doesn't

✅ "AI reads your plans against the NCC. Compliance report out in 10 minutes."
❌ "Our platform leverages advanced AI to streamline your compliance workflow."

✅ "Built by builders, for builders."
❌ "Empowering the next generation of construction professionals."

✅ "Australia's first AI-powered NCC compliance platform."
❌ "The factory that builds AI companies." (← parent-brand voice, do NOT use here)

## Critical guardrail

This product has NO Corporate AI Solutions branding (paying client). Creator voice here is **MMC Build native** — do not bleed in factory/studio/portfolio language under any condition.
